Sat, Jan 16, 2016

GolangBCN's blog released!

We’re really happy to announce this blog’s release, for enhancement of GolangBCN’s group communication. Last year’s ending was plenty of activity, and this blog was a long wanted feature for our group. With the will to bring more activity and freshness to our group, we have finished this one. Might be interesting to mention that the blog is made with Hugo on GitHub Pages. So it’s a static site, which reduces maintenance tasks to a minimum.
